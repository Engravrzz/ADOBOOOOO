Chicken Adobo

Chicken Adobo is a classic Filipino dish made by simmering chicken in soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, and spices. It is savory, slightly tangy, and best enjoyed with steamed rice.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken (cut into serving pieces)
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/3 cup vinegar
  • 1 medium onion (chopped)
  • 4 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 tsp peppercorns
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • Salt and sugar (to taste)

Procedure

  1. In a bowl, combine chicken, soy sauce, and garlic. Marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a pan. Sauté onions and the marinated chicken until lightly browned.
  3. Add vinegar, bay leaves, peppercorns, and water. Do not stir until vinegar has boiled.
  4. Simmer for 30–40 minutes or until chicken is tender and sauce is reduced.
  5. Season with salt and sugar to taste.
  6. Serve hot with steamed rice and enjoy!
